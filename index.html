👋 Welcome to FinTrust Back Office

This back office application stores all client and investment data locally on your device using secure browser storage. No cloud sync. No third-party servers. Your data stays fully under your control.

⚡ Quick Actions

📌 What You Can Do Here

✔ Maintain complete client records
✔ Track SIP, Lumpsum & SWP investments
✔ View consolidated client portfolios
✔ AMC-wise & Scheme-wise summaries
✔ Generate printable investment backups
✔ All data is saved locally in your browser and remains accessible even after closing the app.

🔒 How Local Storage Works

• Data is saved only on this device
• No automatic online synchronization
• No external database or cloud access
• Faster access and instant loading
• Ideal for personal back-office and field usage
⚠️ Important: Clearing browser data or changing devices will remove local records unless you export backups.

💡 Tips

• Use Reports tab to send statements via WhatsApp or Email.
• Export data regularly for safety
• Use the same browser & device for continuity
• Best suited for personal distributor back-office usage
• Optimized for mobile & PWA-style usage
